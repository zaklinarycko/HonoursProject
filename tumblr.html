Tumblr

Threats and Preventions

Phishing scams

Threats

Microsoft, explains that phishing is a kind of “social engineering attack” which consists of using deceiving emails to hoax victims into sharing sensitive information such as passwords, credit card details and other personal information. Those emails are often try to imitate an email from trusted companies to again the trust of the users that are being targeted. The main concept of this attack that instead of targeting the system, it’s the users using these systems are the targets. As seen in the Anti-Phishing Working Group, 2017 report the number of users targeted are very high with number as high as 51, 265 in March of 2017 for number of unique phishing websites detected.

Anti-Phishing Working Group

Prevention

Do not open messages from users that you do not know and do not open any links that they send you. (Avast)

Do not share your information with any one the internet unless you are sure that the source is secure. (Kaspersky Lab)

Do not open any links on Tumblr which you are unsure about and if you do don’t share any personal information on those websites. (Kaspersky Lab)

Do not trust a source on the basis that there are likes and comments on the post, those can be fake comments and likes. (Kaspersky Lab)

Tumblr allows users to report any issues “could jeopardize users’ blogs, expose their personal information, or allow any other nefarious activity to occur.” (Kaspersky Lab)

Human error and not paying attention

Threats

This could include simply posting an accidental post or unknowingly clicking a phishing link. Tumblr also contains adult content or as known on tumblr ‘nsfw’ content which an user can accidently access if clicked on a wrong tag. This also includes not paying attention to your Tumblr. Leaving your profile not monitored could lead to infecting with a virus which t hen could be spread to your friends list. (usecure)

Prevention

Pay attention. Be careful with what you post and what you open. If you do open a link by accident and something unexpected started happening, run your anti-virus to check if a virus wasn’t downloaded onto your device. Tumblr also offers a set of settings that can help stay secure or block certain types of posts. (usecure)
